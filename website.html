Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta with garlic until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Combine pasta with pancetta, then mix in the egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with extra parmesan on top.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g butter
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Beat butter and sugar, then add eggs one at a time.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  5. Pour batter into a greased cake tin.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
